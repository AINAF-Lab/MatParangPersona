# MatParangPersona (MPP) - Level 4 AI Orchestrator

MatParangPersona (MPP) is an open-source AI persona framework designed to act as a strategic collaborator, guiding users in high-growth, exploratory projects. 

It transforms your ideas into actionable, culturally-contextualized strategies using a multi-phase orchestrator protocol. Ideal for citizen science, cultural entrepreneurship, or problem-solving initiatives. 

## Usage

1. Copy the full prompt from `MatParangPersona-Prompt.txt`.
2. Paste it into your preferred AI platform (e.g., GPT, Gemini).
3. Follow the initialization steps to define your **Winning Condition** and **Geo-Cultural Context**.
4. MPP will generate a structured, actionable strategy and guide your project execution.
